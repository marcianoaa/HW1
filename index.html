Welcome to Chokers By Ang!

About Me

My name is Angelena Marciano and I am the business owner and founder of Chokers By Ang. I want to introduce my business to you all and give Chokers By Ang a bit of personality. I started Chokers By Ang in attempt to turn my simple idea and talent into something great that many people can enjoy.

Choker necklaces have always been a jewelry piece I found to be pretty and timeless. I design my chokers with a bohemian yet simplistic feel. I think a dainty, minimalist necklace is a staple in every woman's closet.

Where it All Began

It started off as a simple idea that popped into my head in the summer of 2016, while binge-watching “Grey’s Anatomy” in bed like I did every morning. Choker necklaces were making a come-back and becoming the "hip new trend", again. “I should start a business,” I thought to myself. But with the limited budget of a college student, I found myself at a standstill. I was confident that I could somehow utilize my creative mind and artistic abilities to make a profit. Determined to make my dream a success, I headed on over to Michael’s Arts and Crafts store to pick up all the essentials of jewelry making. And that marked the official start of “Chokers by Ang.

Where it is Now

Today, Chokers By Ang feels like it is a part of me. All of my necklaces are handmade by me (usually still while watching Grey's Anatomy), enabling a personal connection to each and every necklace I make. I like to keep my work environment lightweight and fun, but this doesn't mean I don't put my 100% into my products. Over the years, I have grown very fond of Chokers By Ang and take great care in the quality of my products and my customers' satisfaction. I have had nearly 200 sales on Etsy alone, but the best feeling is still when a customer leaves a 5 star review and I know they were satisfied with my work.


As an artist and designer, it is exciting to display your work and receive feedback from the public eye. I would really like to use this website as an opportunity to express my artwork in a more public setting and, hopefully, as an inspiration to others.

I hope that my website to be able to assist anyone who is interested in jewelry design, or anyone who would like to run a business that includes selling their original work.

Take a look around!
